Structurizr DSL is a text-based notation for describing software architecture models conforming to the C4 model. A workspace file defines the full model — people, systems, containers, components, relationships — and one or more views. The DSL is rendered by the Structurizr browser client or CLI, and supports themes, styles, and auto-layout.

The `@c4-modeler` agent produces DSL exclusively; source files live under `diagrams/` and are version-controlled alongside decisions and radar entries. The heraldry2 and topology themes from [cornifer-contrib](https://github.com/rvr06/cornifer-contrib) are applied for visual consistency.
